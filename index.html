ABOUT US

It was in 2006, with little capital but a pocketful of belief our CEO, Rumman Sadik started Finesta, a software company, right after graduating from HSTU. The new company initially focused on the international market with the local market added in 2010. Since then the company has shown a continuous growth and currently employs over 400+ software engineers. Finesta is now not only an established name in Bangladesh but also in countries like the USA, UK, Netherlands, Denmark, Japan, Norway, Sweden, Germany, Canada, Switzerland, Turkey and the Middle East.
